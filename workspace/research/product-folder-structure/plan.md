# Research Plan: Digital Product Folder Structure Best Practices

**Date:** 2026-03-19
**Scope:** Research best practices for organizing digital products into a standardized folder structure that can handle web apps, static sites, digital downloads, marketing assets, and product metadata.

## Research Question
What are the best practices for organizing digital products (including web apps, static sites, digital downloads, marketing assets) into a standardized folder structure?

## Sub-Questions

1. **Web Application Structure**: What are common folder structures for web applications (Next.js, React, Node.js, Go)? How do they organize source code, public assets, configuration, tests, and documentation?

2. **Digital Product Assets**: What are best practices for organizing digital product assets (marketing materials, screenshots, videos, documentation, templates)? How to separate source files from distribution files?

3. **Product Metadata & Configuration**: How do successful product teams organize product metadata, configuration, deployment files, and environment variables? What patterns exist for separating concerns?

4. **Industry Standards**: What are industry standards for separating code, content, and configuration (e.g., 12-factor app, project structure conventions)? Are there widely adopted conventions?

5. **Multi-Product Consistency**: How to structure for multiple product types (SaaS, digital downloads, templates) while maintaining consistency across the product portfolio?

## Search Strategy

For each sub-question:
- Search GitHub repositories of popular open-source projects
- Look for blog posts and articles about project structure best practices
- Examine documentation from framework creators (Next.js, React, Go)
- Research digital product management practices
- Look for template repositories and boilerplates

## Output
- Raw findings per sub-question in `sq-[N]-findings.md`
- Cross-reference analysis in `cross-ref.md`
- Final report in `report.md`
