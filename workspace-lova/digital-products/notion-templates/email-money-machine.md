# 💡 BEST PRACTICES

## Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use numbers when possible
- Test personal vs. promotional tone
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, $$$, !!!)

## Send Times (Test Your Audience)
- Tuesday 10am - Often best
- Wednesday 2pm - Solid performer
- Thursday 10am - Newsletter day
- Saturday 10am - Relaxed reads

## Body Copy
- First line under 10 words
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Include whitespace
- One clear CTA per email
- P.S. consistently outperforms
