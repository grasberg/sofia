# 📊 CONVERSION PRINCIPLES

## Headline Formulas
- "[Number] Ways to [Desired Outcome]"
- "How to [Achieve Result] Without [Pain Point]"
- "The [System/Framework] That [Achieved X]"
- "[Year]: The Year You [Transformation]"
- "Stop [Problem]. Start [Better Outcome]."

## Body Copy Principles
- Short sentences. Short paragraphs.
- One idea per paragraph.
- Use "you" more than "I/we"
- Paint the before AND after
- Include specific numbers, not vague promises
- Use real stories, not made-up case studies

## CTA Principles
- Make it button-shaped
- Use action verbs: "Get," "Start," "Join," "Download"
- Create urgency without lying
- Give one option, not many

# 🔥 COPY FORMULAS

## PAS (Problem - Agitate - Solution)
1. **[Problem]** "You struggle with [X]"
2. **[Agitate]** "This means you're [worse situation]"
3. **[Solution]** "That's why I created [Y]"

## AIDA (Attention - Interest - Desire - Action)
1. **[Attention]** Hook that stops scrolling
2. **[Interest]** Relevant story or insight
3. **[Desire]** Paint the after-state
4. **[Action]** Clear next step

## BAB (Before - After - Bridge)
1. **[Before]** "You used to [old way]"
2. **[After]** "Now you [new way]"
3. **[Bridge]** "Here's how to get there"

## FAB (Features - Advantages - Benefits)
1. **[Features]** What it has (tool-based)
2. **[Advantages]** How it's better (comparison)
3. **[Benefits]** What it does for THEM (outcome)
