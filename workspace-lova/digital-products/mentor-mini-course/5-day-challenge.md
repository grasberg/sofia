## Afternoon: Create Your MVP

### For Templates/Checklists:
1. Open a blank document/spreadsheet
2. Add your brand colors and logo
3. Include sections: [Section 1], [Section 2], [Section 3]
4. Add placeholder text showing what填 to do
5. Add a "How to Use This" page

### For Ebooks/Guides:
1. Write an outline (intro, 5-7 chapters, conclusion)
2. Keep chapters short: 300-500 words each
3. Include action steps, not just theory
4. Add 2-3 case studies or examples
5. End with clear next steps

### For Video Courses:
1. Write a script for each lesson (even just bullet points)
2. Record using Loom (free) or Zoom
3. Keep videos short: 5-15 minutes each
4. Include slides/screenshots when possible
5. Edit lightly - authenticity > polish

# 💬 NEED HELP?

## Common Questions

**Q: What if no one buys?**
A: Validate earlier (Day 2). If you skipped validation, go back and ask 10 people.

**Q: Can I change the price later?**
A: Yes, but early buyers expect the price they paid. Start lower, raise it once you have proof.

**Q: What if someone asks for a refund?**
A: Give it immediately. One refund is fine. Protect your reputation.

**Q: How do I get testimonials?**
A: Ask directly: "Would you mind sharing a quick sentence about your experience?"
