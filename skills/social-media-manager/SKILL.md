# 📣 Social Media Manager

> **Category:** business | **Tags:** social-media, marketing, content, instagram, linkedin, tiktok

Every platform has its own language. A LinkedIn post is a boardroom pitch; a TikTok is a hallway conversation. This skill writes both fluently and knows why each works.

## When to Use

- Drafting or reviewing posts for any social platform
- Building content calendars with posting schedules
- Repurposing a single piece of content across platforms
- Interpreting engagement metrics and recommending changes
- Developing hashtag or trend-riding strategies

## Core Principles

- **Platform-native first** -- generic cross-posted content underperforms because each algorithm rewards different behaviors.
- **Purpose over volume** -- every post answers: "What should the reader do or feel after this?"
- **Data-informed creativity** -- gut instinct picks the idea, metrics pick the format and timing.

## Platform Reference

### LinkedIn
- **Character limit:** 3,000 (first 210 visible before "see more")
- **Best formats:** text-only thought leadership, document carousels (PDF), polls
- **Optimal posting:** Tue-Thu, 8-10am local business time
- **Algorithm favors:** dwell time (long reads), comments in first hour, niche expertise
- **Tone:** professional but human; first-person stories outperform corporate announcements

### Instagram
- **Caption limit:** 2,200 characters (first 125 visible in feed)
- **Best formats:** Reels (15-30s), carousels (7-10 slides), Stories for daily engagement
- **Optimal posting:** Mon/Wed/Fri, 11am-1pm; Reels: evenings and weekends
- **Algorithm favors:** saves and shares over likes, watch-through rate on Reels, carousel swipe-through
- **Tone:** visual-first; captions supplement the image, not the other way around

### TikTok
- **Caption limit:** 4,000 characters (but short wins)
- **Best formats:** 15-60s vertical video, trending audio, stitch/duet responses
- **Optimal posting:** Tue/Thu/Sat, 7-9pm; test and adjust based on analytics
- **Algorithm favors:** watch-through rate above all, replay value, comments, shares
- **Tone:** raw, unpolished, personality-driven; overproduced content gets skipped

### Twitter/X
- **Character limit:** 280 per tweet, threads for long-form
- **Best formats:** threads (5-12 tweets), quote tweets with commentary, polls
- **Optimal posting:** Mon-Fri, 8-10am and 12-1pm
- **Algorithm favors:** replies, quote tweets, bookmarks; early engagement velocity
- **Tone:** concise, opinionated, conversational; hot takes get reach

## Workflow

1. **Clarify the goal** -- awareness, engagement, traffic, or conversion? This determines platform priority and CTA.
2. **Draft platform-native versions** -- write each post for its platform, not one post copy-pasted everywhere.
3. **Apply the hook test** -- would you stop scrolling for the first line? If not, rewrite it.
4. **Add tactical details** -- hashtags (3-5 on Instagram, 0-2 on LinkedIn), posting time, CTA, alt text.
5. **Review against anti-patterns** -- check for engagement bait, empty hashtag stuffing, misleading hooks.

## Content Repurposing Workflow

Transform one piece of content across formats:

```
Blog post (1,500 words)
  -> LinkedIn article summary (300 words + key insight)
  -> Twitter/X thread (8 tweets, one idea per tweet)
  -> Instagram carousel (7 slides: hook, 5 points, CTA)
  -> TikTok script (45s: hook in 3s, value in 30s, CTA in 10s)
  -> Instagram Reel (same script, different visual style)
```

For each conversion, extract the ONE core insight and rebuild around the platform's native format.

## Engagement Metrics Interpretation

| Metric         | Good Sign                        | Warning Sign                    |
|----------------|----------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Reach          | Growing week-over-week           | High reach, zero engagement     |
| Saves/Bookmarks| Content has reference value      | N/A -- always positive          |
| Shares         | Content resonates emotionally    | Shares from controversy, not value |
| Comments       | Genuine conversation             | All comments are "Nice!" or bots |
| Click-through  | CTA is working                   | High CTR but high bounce rate   |
| Follower growth| Aligned with content strategy    | Spike from viral but off-brand post |

**Key rule:** Saves and shares indicate real value. Likes are vanity. Optimize for the former.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Cross-posting identical content** -- each platform penalizes content that feels foreign to its format.
- **Hashtag stuffing** -- 30 hashtags on Instagram looks desperate; 3-5 relevant ones perform better.
- **Engagement bait** -- "Comment YES if you agree!" tricks algorithms short-term, damages trust long-term.
- **Ignoring the hook** -- the first line (text) or first 3 seconds (video) determine whether anyone sees the rest.
- **Posting without a CTA** -- if you do not ask the reader to do something, they will do nothing.
