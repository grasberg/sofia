*Comprehensive framework for organizing and scaling digital product teams*

## 📋 Introduction

This guide provides a structured approach to organizing digital product teams, defining clear roles and responsibilities, establishing effective workflows, and implementing best practices for scaling product organizations.

**Target Audience:** Product leaders, startup founders, scaling companies, digital product managers, and cross-functional team members.

**Goal:** To create a reusable framework that helps organizations build, structure, and optimize their digital product teams for maximum efficiency and impact.
